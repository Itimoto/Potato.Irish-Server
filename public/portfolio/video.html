e-prevent.

e-prevent was a submission video for Samsung’s Solve-For-Tomorrow Competition, presenting a vape-detecting sensor.
Though we did not move on to become Finalists, the video still serves as an attempt to balance both the persuasive and informational aspects of a product designed to solve a pervasive issue in a community – and, most importantly, presents the culmination of our attempts to curb it.

Technical issues still permeate the video, however – in part due to the fact that it was filmed entirely on a Samsung Smartphone, limiting the Camera’s dynamic range, among other things…
We did the best with what we had.

Roles – Mentor, Co-Director, Editor

GHS’ Got Talent Show

In the starting phases of the COVID outbreak, towards the end of my Senior Year, Green High School’s Key Club was planning on keeping up their long-running, annual tradition that was the Talent Show
They reached out to me for help with some video/technical details, we put together an editing team, and we made the best with what we had – there were a number of technical challenges involved here, and it is by far the longest production I have been involved.
